Soccer Skills with Steven Gerrard

Welcome students and parents! My name is Steven Gerrard. I am the soccer skills professor here at School of Sporting Knowledge. If your child is in the soccer path, I will be teaching them all I know about the beautiful game.

About Me

I, myself, used to play professional soccer in the English Premier League and MLS. For the bulk of my career, I played for Liverpool Football Club. While I was there I won trophies including the Champions League, Europa League, FA Cup, and League Cup. I am now retired, and I hope to use my experience to enhance my student's ability in terms of the physical game itself.

Rules and Expectations

    • Students are expected to arrive to training on time, or they will be permitted to do fitness instead.
    • Any slander or criticism of other students' skills will lead to disciplinary action.
    • I expect students to be in the gear that is necessary each training session.
    • Students' progression and participation will determine their grade.
    • If a student does not follow the guidelines or perform to my standards, they will be removed from the program.
