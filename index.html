Student and Athlete of the Month

Congratulations to Rashad Evans and Michael Downtree for being our Student Athletes of the month. For the month of February, no one has represented our school better. Both students averaged the best test scores for this month with an average score of 98. Rashad went for six consecutive triple doubles during our basketball team's title run while Michael carried our wrestling team to a state championship!

Community Service Info

A broad-based community advisory group comprised of parents, teachers, and community members has been established in every school to work with the principal to cultivate a positive school environment supporting student success. As an advisory body, the SIC provides input, feedback, and recommendations to support the school.
